FIZZBUZZARD FEED

TopWorstDecisions

By Roddir

7. Letting Ludwig (and Ryan) Out Of Sight

We probably shouldn't do this ever again, and in our defense, we did not expect things to go that poorly. Still a really bad call. To be fair, we didn't know that would or even could happen, and now that we do, we don't let it happen again.

6. Buying Ringo

I don't think this was an actual horse. We've met lots of horses and this one loved horse-crack way more than any other horse we've met. I'm really glad we sold it to the bone mill and it won't come back in the future. I really didn't like this thing, but I'm glad it's not reccuring and all of our other horses are normal.

5. Buying Percy

I do think this is an actual bird, but also like, it's a really weird one. Billy never seems to know if it's there or not, and it's a little strange how "free reign" it is. It's kinda cute that it knows how to read and write, though. I wish it knew how to draw. I think it could make cute drawings. The only reason Percy is higher on the list is because he's still around.

4. Molotov-ing The Tower

I don't enjoy burnt hair. I especially don't like burning my own hair. I also don't like getting knocked out while my hair is burning. It's not a pleasant smell to die to. Especially when people on our side were in there. Like me.

3. Making a Deal with Moon God During Mindflayer Fight

I hate debts. I hate gods. This was a really bad time. I feel like we had things mostly handled, even with Kitt strapped in a chair. And the Castel's mom stabbing us. And the really devastating stun on over half the party. We were perfectly on par for the course. No gods needed.

2. Sending Racoons to Rivercross

These guys are little menaces. It's a miracle the city is still standing. I don't know if peace was ever an option, but I wish we could've found it without sacrificing a city. RIP Suzanne and her muffins. I don't know if she's dead, but it's likely, right?

1. Asking for Clemency for Various Crimes for Winning The Night Festival Drinking Contest

In retrospect, and as a "law professional", drunks aren't usually granted pardons. While we were granted a favor, it probably wouldn't cover our multitude of crimes. Maybe just one crime if we only did one crime, but boy howdy.
